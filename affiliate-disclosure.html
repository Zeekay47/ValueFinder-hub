Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how we make money and our commitment to unbiased recommendations.

Quick Summary

ValueFinder Hub is an affiliate marketing website. We earn commissions when you click on our product links and make purchases. This comes at no extra cost to you and helps us maintain our free service.

What is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based marketing arrangement where we earn commissions on sales generated through our referral links. When you click on a product link on our website and make a purchase on the retailer's website, the retailer pays us a small commission.

This commission comes at no additional cost to you - the price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer's website.

Our Editorial Independence

Important Commitment

Our affiliate relationships do NOT influence our product recommendations. We maintain strict editorial independence and recommend products based on their merits, not on which products earn us the highest commissions.

How We Maintain Objectivity

  • We use data-driven analysis to compare products
  • We consider customer reviews from multiple sources
  • We analyze price trends and historical data
  • We test products when possible (or rely on expert reviews)
  • We disclose when we have a financial relationship with a brand

What We Never Do

  • We never recommend inferior products for higher commissions
  • We never accept payments for positive reviews
  • We never hide negative aspects of products
  • We never manipulate prices or reviews
  • We never compromise our integrity for financial gain

Commission Rates

Commission rates vary by retailer and product category. Typical commission rates range from 1% to 10% of the purchase price. Some programs offer flat-rate commissions per sale.

Here are approximate commission rates for major programs:

Retailer Typical Commission Rate Payment Terms
Amazon Associates 1-10% depending on category Monthly, 60-day hold
Walmart Affiliates 1-4% depending on category Monthly
Target Affiliates 1-8% depending on category Monthly
Best Buy Affiliates 1-6% depending on category Monthly

Note: These rates are subject to change and may vary based on product category, promotional periods, and other factors. The exact commission rate for each product is determined by the retailer.

Cookie Duration

When you click on an affiliate link, a cookie is placed in your browser that identifies us as the referring website. These cookies have different durations:

24h

24-Hour Cookies

Some programs only pay commissions on purchases made within 24 hours of clicking our link.

7d

7-Day Cookies

Most programs offer 7-day cookies for commission tracking.

30d

30-Day Cookies

Some programs offer extended 30-day cookie durations.

This means if you click on one of our links and make a purchase within the cookie duration window, we'll receive a commission even if you don't buy immediately. Some programs also track subsequent purchases made during the cookie period.

Your Privacy

When you click on an affiliate link, the retailer may collect information about your visit. We do not have access to your personal information (name, address, payment details) when you make a purchase through our links.

The retailer only shares aggregated, non-personal information with us (e.g., "3 sales were made through your links today totaling $450").

For more information about how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.

FTC Compliance

FTC Compliance: We comply with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines for affiliate marketing disclosures. We clearly and conspicuously disclose our affiliate relationships as required by law.

According to FTC guidelines, we must:

  • Clearly disclose our affiliate relationships
  • Place disclosures where they're easily noticeable
  • Use clear and understandable language
  • Disclose on each page containing affiliate links

That's why you'll see our disclosure notice throughout our website, including on every product page and in our footer.
